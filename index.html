How it works

From presentation
to clarity, together

MedAxis works the way you think — iteratively, in conversation, with the full clinical picture always in view.

1
Present the case

Type or upload the patient's history, symptoms, vitals, and any available results — in your own words, no structured forms required.

2
Get ranked differentials

MedAxis returns a prioritised differential list with probability estimates, clinical reasoning, and the evidence supporting each diagnosis.

3
Explore and challenge

Ask follow-up questions. Add new data. Drill into complications, dangerous mimics, or treatment contraindications — the conversation refines the picture.

4
Act with confidence

Receive suggested investigations, treatment pathways, referral criteria, and red flags — all grounded in current clinical guidelines.

Trust & safety

Built for
clinical reality

MedAxis is designed for the accountability of medicine — transparent, traceable, and clear about what it is.

Clinician always decides

MedAxis is a reasoning aid, not an autonomous system. Every output is an input to your clinical judgement — never a replacement for it.

Full reasoning trace

Every diagnosis comes with the clinical logic behind it — which symptoms, findings, and risk factors drove the output.

Session-only data

No patient data is stored. Each session is ephemeral — what you enter stays in the session and is not retained or used for training.
